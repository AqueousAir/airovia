Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AIROVIA collects, uses, and protects information submitted through this website. AIROVIA is committed to handling information responsibly and transparently.

1. Information We Collect

AIROVIA collects only limited information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name and professional contact details (e.g., email address)
  • Organization, project location, and use-case context
  • Messages or inquiries submitted through contact forms or email

AIROVIA does not collect sensitive personal data through this website.

2. How We Use Information

Information you submit is used solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries and requests for information
  • Evaluating potential project relevance and deployment context
  • Maintaining professional communication records

AIROVIA does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

3. Data Storage & Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied to protect submitted information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

While AIROVIA takes appropriate precautions, no internet-based transmission can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

4. Cookies & Analytics

This website does not currently use cookies for user profiling or behavioral tracking. Basic technical analytics may be used in the future to understand website performance and usage patterns.

5. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to address inquiries, maintain records of professional communications, or meet legitimate business or legal requirements.

6. Third-Party Services

Communications may be processed using standard email or hosting service providers. AIROVIA is not responsible for the privacy practices of external platforms linked or referenced on this website.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your submitted information by contacting AIROVIA. Requests will be handled within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. Updates will be posted on this page.
